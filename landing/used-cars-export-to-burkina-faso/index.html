What Your Quotation Will Show

A useful quotation identifies the vehicle model and version, stock status, unit price, applicable inspection service, export preparation, named departure point and validity period. For CIF requests it also names the destination port and the freight basis used on the quotation date. Optional services are shown separately. This structure helps an importer see whether two offers cover the same work and prevents destination charges from being confused with China-side export costs.

Stock is reserved only under the terms stated in the commercial offer. Before reservation, color, configuration and condition remain subject to availability. We recommend that buyers avoid making retail promises in their local market until the exact unit and route are confirmed. During preparation, the buyer receives the available progress evidence and can contact the export team through WhatsApp for operational updates.

Order Planning and Cost Control

A reliable export order needs more than a headline vehicle price. Buyers should compare the exact trim, production year, mileage, visible condition, inspection scope, China inland delivery, port charges, freight basis and destination costs. Our quotation identifies the named vehicle and the services included so that an FOB offer can be compared fairly with a CIF offer. If stock changes before confirmation, we submit a replacement option for approval rather than changing the unit without notice.

Before payment, the buyer should provide the legal company or importer details required for the transaction and confirm that the selected model is eligible at destination. After confirmation, we coordinate vehicle handover, inspection evidence, export preparation and transport booking. Copies of available documents and shipment information are shared during the process. Buyers should appoint a qualified customs broker at destination and allow for possible carrier schedule changes.

For repeat purchasing, send a preferred model range, maximum landed-cost target and monthly quantity. This allows our sourcing team to screen ready stock more efficiently. For a first order, a precise one-unit request is usually more effective than a broad request for the cheapest car. Clear specifications reduce substitutions, shorten quotation time and make inspection approval easier.
