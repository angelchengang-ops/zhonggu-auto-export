Export Process

  1. Confirm target model, quantity, destination and budget.
  2. Prepare FOB quotation and available stock options.
  3. Coordinate inspection, documents and export preparation.
  4. Arrange customs declaration and shipping coordination.
  5. Support delivery updates until loading or handover.

Documents and Shipping Support

Our team supports export invoice preparation, vehicle information checks, inspection coordination, customs declaration communication and port loading coordination. Exact document requirements depend on vehicle type and destination market, so every inquiry is confirmed before quotation.
